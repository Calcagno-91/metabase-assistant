Privacy Policy – Metabase Assistant

Metabase Assistant is a browser extension designed to help users create dashboards and cards in Metabase more efficiently.

What Information We Collect

This extension does not collect or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

How We Use Information

The extension runs locally in your browser. It only interacts with the Metabase pages open in your browser and stores temporary settings or preferences using chrome.storage. It does not send any data to third-party services unless you explicitly configure it to connect to your own Metabase instance or local AI server.

Permissions Explained

  • storage: used to save extension preferences locally.
  • scripting, content_scripts: used to interact with Metabase pages only.
  • <all_urls>: needed to support any custom-hosted Metabase instance (e.g., company or local environments).

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.
